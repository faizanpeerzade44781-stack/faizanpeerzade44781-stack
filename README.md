## 📌 About Me

Data Analyst with 2 years of experience turning raw, messy datasets into KPIs, dashboards, and decisions stakeholders can act on. Core stack: **SQL, Python (Pandas/NumPy), Power BI, and Excel**, applied across retail, e-commerce, and financial-services datasets. I own the full analytics lifecycle — cleaning and validating data, engineering metrics, building interactive dashboards, and writing the business recommendation that follows from the numbers. Every project I ship pairs a technical workflow with a stated business problem and a concrete next action, because a dashboard without a decision attached to it isn't finished. Based in Bangalore, currently focused on SQL performance, DAX, and automating recurring reporting.

|---|---|
| **Business Problem** | Leadership had no consolidated view of loan portfolio health or credit risk exposure — decisions were being made branch-by-branch instead of data-driven. |
